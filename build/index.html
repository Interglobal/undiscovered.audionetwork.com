The Undiscovered Series is a new initiative showcasing some of the brightest new talent in music.

Releasing a regular stream of innovative new music from dynamic and talented composers, it will feature brand new albums from up to four of our most exciting new writers at a time, covering an eclectic range of genres.

Each album will be included in the Audio Network catalogue, ready for use in TV, film, advertising and corporate video.

Want to be on the next Undiscovered album?

We’re already looking for young and talented composers for our next Undiscovered release.

If you would like to be considered, or know someone who might, get in touch with our A&R department on: aandr@audionetwork or 01787 477 277
